# Securing the Smile: Why Dental Systems Must Adopt Sovereign Security

**By Samuel Muriithi Gitandu (The Peculiar Librarian)**

## The Vulnerability of Centralized Health
In 2026, dental practices often rely on third-party SaaS providers that prioritize subscription billing over data integrity. When these systems go down or change terms, the clinical record—the "Smile's History"—is held hostage.

## The PADI Solution
The **Practice-Area Depth Index (PADI)** introduces a deterministic approach to clinical data. By using SHACL-driven validation, we ensure that:
1. **Ownership is Local:** The practitioner owns the raw RDF graph.
2. **Integrity is Absolute:** Every clinical event is hashed and immutable.
3. **Sovereignty is Maintained:** No "Digital Rent" is required to access your own professional truth.
